Hey Product Hunt! 👋

I'm yuangui, and I've been building xyvaClaw for the past 6 months.

The idea started simple: I wanted an AI assistant that actually DOES things, not just chats. Something that could automate my daily workflows — from generating documents to managing my Feishu/Lark workspace to screening stocks.

What makes xyvaClaw different:

🔄 **Self-Evolution** — It learns from its mistakes. After a month of use, it's noticeably smarter than day one. It records errors, tracks outcomes of every decision, and periodically reflects on its behavior patterns.

🧠 **Lossless Context** — Most AI assistants "forget" earlier parts of long conversations. Our custom engine preserves all critical information regardless of conversation length.

⚡ **38 Built-in Skills** — Browser automation, Word/Excel generation, AI video creation, A-share stock screening, social media management, and more. All pre-installed.

🛡️ **5-Level Fallback** — DeepSeek → Qwen → Kimi → Reasoner → Qwen Max. If one API goes down, it auto-switches. Zero downtime in 6 months.

💬 **Deep Feishu/Lark** — 112 TypeScript files covering nearly every Feishu API. Manage docs, tables, calendars, approvals — all through chat.

It's fully open source (MIT), runs on your machine, and costs almost nothing (DeepSeek gives free credits on signup).

Would love to hear your feedback! 🙏
